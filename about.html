Elite Restaurant

Elite Restaurant is located in Nablus, on the first floor of the Municipality Complex, and it opened in 2010.
This restaurant offers a variety of delicious dishes, including Italian cuisine, traditional meals, and various natural juices.
It is known for its quiet atmosphere, excellent service, and great prices.
